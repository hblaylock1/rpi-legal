Privacy Policy — Pipeline App

Effective date: June 1, 2026 · Last updated: June 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Romans Precision Irrigation ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Pipeline mobile application (the "App") on Android devices.

Pipeline is an internal business application provided to authorized employees and staff of Romans Precision Irrigation for the purpose of accessing company business systems (including Sage 100 ERP and related operational tools). The App is not intended for use by the general public, customers, or anyone outside authorized personnel.

1. Information We Collect

To operate the App, we collect and process the following categories of information:

  • Account credentials — your username, password, and any related authentication information you provide to sign in.
  • Profile information — name, email address, role, and other employment-related identifiers required to associate App usage with your authorized account.
  • Device and push notification data — a Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) registration token used solely to deliver push notifications to your device, along with basic device identifiers (operating system version, app version) needed for compatibility and troubleshooting.
  • App usage and business data — information you create or access through the App in the course of your work, including but not limited to quotes, orders, inventory, purchase orders, customer records, service tickets, time entries, and related business documents.
  • Technical logs — IP address, timestamps, request data, and error logs collected for security, debugging, and operational reliability.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information described above only for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate you and authorize access to company systems.
  • To provide the App's core functionality and connect it to internal business systems.
  • To deliver push notifications related to business events (e.g., order updates, assignments, approvals).
  • To maintain security, prevent unauthorized access, and investigate misuse.
  • To diagnose technical issues and improve the App.

We do not sell, rent, or otherwise share your personal information with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. We do not use your information to build advertising profiles.

3. Third-Party Services

The App relies on the following third-party service providers, who process limited information strictly on our behalf:

  • Google Firebase Cloud Messaging — used to deliver push notifications. Google processes the device registration token and notification payload. See Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Sage 100 ERP — the App connects to our internal Sage 100 business system, which stores authorized business data on infrastructure we control.
  • Operational integrations — depending on your role, the App may transmit data to internal integrations including Microsoft SharePoint, Square, Ecwid, and ClockShark, each governed by the contracts we maintain with those providers and their respective privacy policies.

4. Data Retention

We retain account credentials and business data for as long as you are an authorized user and as required by applicable law and our internal record-keeping policies. Push notification tokens are removed when you log out of the App or uninstall it. Server logs are retained for a limited operational window and then deleted or anonymized.

5. Data Security

We protect data in transit using HTTPS/TLS and restrict access to authorized personnel only. The App enforces secure network configuration and does not transmit data over unencrypted connections. While we take reasonable measures to safeguard information, no system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

As an authorized user, you may request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information we hold about you, subject to applicable law and our legitimate business and record-retention obligations. To make a request, contact us using the information below.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children and is intended solely for use by adult employees authorized by Romans Precision Irrigation. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 16 years of age.

8. Permissions Used by the App

The Pipeline Android app requests the following permissions:

  • INTERNET & ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE — required to communicate with company servers.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — required to display push notifications you have opted into.
  • DOWNLOAD_WITHOUT_NOTIFICATION — used to download business documents (e.g., PDFs) within the App.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Material changes will be communicated through the App or via your authorized contact channels.
